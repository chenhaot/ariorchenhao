How to Play

Every week, Ari and Chenhao each submit 3 hot takes. Your job is to guess who said what!

  • Read each hot take casually
  • Click "Ari" or "Chenhao" to make your guess
  • See how much each hot take resonates with you
  • Come back each week for new hot takes

What's a Hot Take?

A hot take is a bold, sometimes controversial opinion. It's meant to provoke thought and discussion. We focus on AI hot takes, maybe also academia and life. It is mostly for fun!

The Rules

  • Each week features 6 hot takes (3 from each person)
  • Hot takes are shuffled randomly
  • No hints, just pure intuition
  • To make this game fair, we promise that we believe each hot take, at least to some extent. We are not making up opinions for the sake of the game.

Who are Ari and Chenhao?

We are Professors of Computer Science at the University of Chicago.
